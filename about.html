Who We Are

One Stop Solution for All Your Needs.

At BS Realty & Mortgage, we take pride in delivering personalized, high-quality services that cater to your unique needs. With a deep understanding of the real estate and financial sectors, our goal is to ensure your satisfaction at every step, making your experience efficient, informed, and hassle-free. Let us be your partner in achieving your real estate and financial goals.

  • Real Estate: We assist with residential and commercial property transactions, offering expert advice and support whether you're buying, selling, or renting.
  • Mortgages: Our team provides tailored mortgage solutions to help you find the best financing options, from first-time homebuyer programs to refinancing.
  • Tax & Accounting: We offer consulting services that streamline your tax planning and financial management, ensuring your real estate decisions are financially sound.
  • Utility Setup: We simplify the process of setting up utilities in your new home, from electricity to internet, ensuring a smooth transition.
  • Consulting: Whether you need advice on real estate investments, financial planning, or tax strategies, we offer expert consultation to guide your decisions.
  • Home Improvement: From plumbing and electrical work to complete renovations, we provide home improvement services that enhance the value and comfort of your property.
